Karachi:
Karachi is the capital city of the Pakistani province of Sindh. It is the largest city in Pakistan and 12th largest in the world.the biggest metropolitan city of Pakistan, is known for its diverse culture, advanced education system, rich cuisine, historical sites, best shopping malls, top cinemas and various farmhouses, and, of course, the beautiful deep blue sea.
